Cloudy with a chance of Meatballs

Cloudy with a Chance of Meatballs is a science fiction comedy film based on a children's book of the same name. The film focuses on an aspiring inventor named Flint Lockwood, who, after many failed experiments, creates a machine that can convert water into food.
